Limited beta coming soon

Posted on Jan 6, 2015 by Phil Hawksworth

We are busy beavering away on our first release of Kanzan. Before long we will be inviting a few people to join our private beta.

We’ll be starting small, but we like small. We think that Kanzan can be useful even with a limited initial feature set. In fact we’ll be aiming to keep things rather small and focussed even after we launch.

If you’d like to be a part of the invitation-only beta, or to get updates on progress as platform evolves towards its first release, then head on over to kanzan.io and sign up for more information. We’ll keep you in the loop.
